Shared responsibility

Responsibility split

AWS handles

  • Physical datacenters, power, and hardware protection
  • Availability of foundational cloud services
  • Managed regional and Availability Zone infrastructure

Customer handles

  • Operating system patching and host configuration
  • IAM design, MFA policy, and role assignment
  • Application hardening, encryption choices, and data controls

Recognition guide

What students should notice

  • Global search, region selectors, and account context stay visible across the top.
  • Services are grouped by domain such as compute, networking, storage, and identity.
  • Operational dashboards combine metrics, resource tables, alarms, and cost summaries.
  • Most administration tasks move between resource inventory, security controls, logs, and cost views.
