Shared responsibility

Responsibility split

AWS handles

  • Physical facilities and hardware security
  • Availability of foundational cloud services
  • Global infrastructure and region operations

Customer handles

  • Operating system patching
  • IAM users and access policies
  • Application configuration and data protection

Recognition guide

What students should notice

  • The top bar usually carries account, region, and search tools.
  • The left navigation groups services and recently visited resources.
  • Dashboard cards summarize health, capacity, and costs at a glance.
  • Tables, metrics, and alerts are central to routine administration.
